The International Cultural Art & Property Exchange


The International Cultural Art & Property Exchange ("ICAPE"), is a specialized exchange specifically tailored for collectible (arts and antique) owners and investors. ICAPE is a full service exchange that provides origination/issuance, primary/secondary market trading, and record-keeping for transacting fractional interests in physical collectibles. ICAPE provides an invaluable venue for art and antique collectors to materialize the value of their collections and creates market liquidity for hard-to-value items.

The exchange helps owner as well as investor securitize their items in fractional interest units pursuant to a listing agreement, and subsequently actively involves itself in trading and making markets for the listed item in order to promote fair and transparent market valuation for illiquid collectibles. The ICAPE solution, similar to the standard of scholarly connoisseurship conducted by large art auction houses, is a new generation electronic trading environment that boasts reliability, high performance, information transparency and market depth. It represents the future of electronic trading of all hard-to-value collectibles.
