C2Class


Well aware that good education is the foundation of any society, MLA Group strives to provide the most cutting-edge online platform for such educational programs as early childcare, K-12 education consulting, higher education enrollment consulting, corporate trainings, global education and culture exchange conferences/events.

The firm’s integrated online education environment, C2Class.com,utilizing the most advanced technology, delivers the ultimate learning experience catered to each learner’s individual needs. C2Class offers state-of-the-art online learning tools and features, including interactive virtual classroom, instant video notes, real time learning community, portal search engine, international teaching assistant program, and so on. MLA's mission is to provide the most effective and personalized educational services to students and learners of all ages and levels around the world.
