Who we are? 

MLA Technology Group Corp is an international, diversified technology holding company focused on creating and delivering innovative internet and mobile software products and solutions to customers around the world. The company has had tremendous success in developing businesses in broad spectrum of industries including education, entertainment, financial services and online content distribution. Headquartered in the Financial District of New York City, MLA also established a branch office in Beijing, China. Geographically, MLA Group has extensive business coverage in North America, Europe and Asia (Russia, China, and India).
